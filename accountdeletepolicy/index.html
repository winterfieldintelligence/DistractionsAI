Account Deletion Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

This page explains how account deletion works in the Distractions AI Android app.

1. How to delete your account

  • Open the app and log in.
  • Go to the Imagine page sidebar.
  • Tap Delete Account (red button) and confirm.

2. What is deleted immediately

  • Your account record (email and hashed password) from local app database.
  • Your active session data.
  • Your recent history data stored in app preferences.

3. What is not deleted automatically

  • Images already downloaded to your device storage (Downloads/DistractionsAI).

You can manually remove downloaded files from your device file manager at any time.

4. After deletion

You are logged out and returned to the Login/Signup screen. You can create a new account later using the app.
