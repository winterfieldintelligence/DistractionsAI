Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Distractions AI (Developer: Eknath Patil) respects your privacy. This page explains what data the app handles and why.

1. Data we process

  • Account credentials (email + hashed password) stored in local app database.
  • Note browsing history stored in local app preferences.
  • Downloaded files saved only when user taps download.

2. How data is used

Data is used to authenticate users, show selected notes, and keep recent history in app.

3. Network and third parties

The app loads note images from a public CDN/GitHub source you configured. No ad SDK or analytics SDK is included in this app build.

4. Data sharing

Distractions AI does not sell personal data. Data is not shared except where required by law.

5. User controls

You can delete your account from the in-app sidebar (Delete Account) or clear app data from device settings to remove locally stored account and history data.
