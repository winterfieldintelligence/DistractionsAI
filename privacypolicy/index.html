Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Distractions AI ("App") is developed and maintained by Eknath Patil. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles data.

1. Information We Collect

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Email address and hashed password (used for login authentication)
  • Note browsing history
  • Downloaded files (only when you manually choose to download content)

All information is stored locally on your device.
The App does not transmit or store personal data on any developer-controlled servers.

2. Permissions Used

Notification Permission (Android 13+)
This permission is requested only to display download progress notifications.
The App does not send marketing, promotional, or unrelated notifications.

3. Network Usage and Third-Party Services

The App loads note images and related content from publicly available content delivery networks (CDN) or GitHub-hosted sources.

When accessing such content, your device may automatically share technical information (such as IP address) with those third-party services. The developer does not control third-party server logs or their data practices.

The App does not include:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Analytics SDKs
  • Tracking tools

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties.
Data is not shared except if required by applicable law.

5. Data Retention

All stored data remains on your device until:

  • You delete your account within the App
  • You clear app data from your device settings
  • You uninstall the App

Clearing app data or uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored information.

6. Security

We implement standard security practices, including password hashing, to protect locally stored account credentials.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed to children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights and Controls

You may:

  • Delete your account within the App
  • Clear app data from your device
  • Uninstall the App at any time

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
